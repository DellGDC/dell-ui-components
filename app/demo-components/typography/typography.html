Heading modification: H5 desktop and h5 tablet may take a bold styling if page hierarchy requires it. All other headings may not be modified. For example, if a heading is set in Museo Sans for Dell 300, do not change it to Museo Sans for Dell 700.

Usage guidance: For additional usage guidelines around typography, please see the Typography Guidelines at http://www.delldesignlibrary.com/guides/typography.html.

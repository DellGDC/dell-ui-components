Columns

Inside each column is 15px of padding placed on the left and right ( See the light gray shaded area ). This padding pushes the live area in by a total of 30px per column. The "Gutter" is created by the sum of the padding between two adjacent columns.

Inside Live Area for a Column

The live content area for each column is indicated by the light yellow shaded area with red dotted lines.
